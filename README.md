**Licheng Town First Primary School – Where Young Minds Blossom**

📖 [中文版本](./README_zh.md) | English version below

Located in the heart of Lipu City, Guilin, Guangxi, Licheng Town First Primary School (Licheng Yixiao) stands as a beacon of educational excellence with a proud history spanning over 85 years. From our two campuses, we have been nurturing generations of learners, building a legacy of knowledge, character, and innovation.

**A Campus Built for Learning**

Our main campus spans 26,640 square meters, with over 22,840 square meters of modern facilities. We house 36 classes and serve 2,451 students, supported by a dedicated team of 124 highly qualified teachers. Our advanced resources – including multimedia networks, computer labs, a language lab, a library, and science laboratories – meet the standards of a model school, ensuring our students learn in an environment that inspires curiosity and discovery.

**Educating the Whole Child**

Guided by the philosophy of "experimentation in education, scientific management, and research-driven development," we are committed to holistic growth. Our approach integrates moral, intellectual, physical, aesthetic, and labor education. Through innovative teaching, research-led instruction, and vibrant extracurricular activities, we cultivate not only academic excellence but also creativity, resilience, and social responsibility.

**A National Leader in Character Education**

We are proud to be recognized as a **National Defense Education Model School** – a testament to our commitment to fostering patriotism and civic duty. Our students participate in flag guard demonstrations, military-style exercises, and cultural activities that blend discipline with creativity. This distinctive program has made us a regional leader, hosting city-wide education conferences and welcoming educators from across Guangxi.

**A Tradition of Excellence**

Our commitment to quality has earned us numerous accolades, including:
- Guangxi Modern Educational Technology Experimental School
- Guangxi Innovative Education Experimental School
- Guangxi Activity Curriculum Experimental School
- Guangxi Audio-Visual Education Experimental School
- Autonomous Region Table Tennis Traditional School
- Guilin Education Research Model School
- Guilin Arts Education Model School

We have also been recognized as a Guilin Municipal "Civilized Campus" and an International Eco-School, reflecting our commitment to sustainability and community values.

**Join Our Community**

At Licheng Town First Primary School, we believe every child deserves a foundation for lifelong success. We invite families to discover a school where tradition meets innovation, and where each student is empowered to grow, lead, and thrive.
